Cheetah

As the fastest animal on land, cheetahs can run at a speed of 120 kilometers per hour.They can finish a 100 meter dash in 6 seconds,while the man's 100m record is 9.58 seconds.

  • Scientific Name:Acinonyx jubatus
  • Average Length:1-1.5m
  • Average Lifespan:8-12years
  • Habitat:Temperate and tropical grassland

Cheetahs have black spots all over the body,the pale yellow hair and black spots help cheetahs camouflage on the grassland.They have black stripes from the mouth to the corner of their eyes,which is a feature different from the leopard.
